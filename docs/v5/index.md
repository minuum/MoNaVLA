## 3. Analysis of Breakthroughs

### 🚀 Shift from Regression to Discrete (Exp 01 & 02)
- **Problem**: V4 regression always predicted "Forward" in ambiguous turns (averaging).
- **Solution**: Forced the model to choose one of the predefined classes.
- **Finding**: Class imbalance (75% Forward) still caused a bias, leading to the removal of straight-path data in Exp 02.

### 🧠 Semantic Constraint (Exp 03)
- **Discovery**: The model recognized "Gray Basket" but didn't know "Left/Right" instructions.
- **Innovation**: Added **CLIP Norm Loss** to force the action features to stay close to the CLIP Text Embeddings of the instructions.
- **Result**: Drastic reduction in loss and better alignment with user commands.

### 🏗️ Foundation Weight Criticality (Exp 04)
- **Discovery**: V4 checkpoints were "polluted" with overfitted vision-to-action features.
- **Solution**: Switched to a clean **Google-Robot pretrained** foundation.
- **Result**: Visual grounding capabilities were restored and learning speed tripled.

### 🎯 Goal-Oriented Instruction (Exp 08 & 09)
- **Optimization**: Changed instructions from "Navigate" to "**Navigate until centered in the frame**".
- **Result**: The model learned the *termination condition* (STOP) much more effectively as the basket reached the center.
